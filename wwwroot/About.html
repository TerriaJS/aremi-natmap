AREMI is a website for map-based access to Australian spatial data relevant to the Renewable Energy industry - with a focus on Developers, Financiers, and Policy Makers. It is funded by the Australian Renewable Energy Agency and developed by NICTA in partnership with the Clean Energy Council with hosting being provided by Geoscience Australia. This website is an extension of the Australian Government's National Map initiative.

AREMI is a fully open architecture. When you access data through it, you are typically accessing the data directly from the government department or agency which is the custodian of that data.

AREMI...

  • provides easy access to authoritative and other spatial data to government, business and the public
  • facilitates the opening of data by federal, state and local government bodies
  • provides an open framework of geospatial data services that supports commercial and community innovation

To see what data is available on AREMI, refer to the Data Catalogue in the AREMI itself. Click the Data Catalogue tab and expand the Data Set group names.

How to use AREMI

Just go to http://nationalmap.gov.au/renewables/. Most operations are obvious, but you can click the Help button for specific instructions.

For further information email aremi@nicta.com.au. Feel free to send us feedback and bug reports.

Contributing Data to AREMI

If you have a dataset you think would suit AREMI then please get in contact with us: aremi@nicta.com.au

AREMI Software

The AREMI is an Open Source system available as a Github project. The core software of this system was initially developed by NICTA for the National Map, but has subsequently been used for other projects. It has been made available to assist with the development of other spatial data viewing applications.
